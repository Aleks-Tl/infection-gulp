About Us

We help frustrated facility managers who want an evidence-based cleaning solution to the threat of microbial contamination, so they can feel safe and focus on delivering high quality service to their customers.

We do this by providing top-notch teams of highly skilled cleaning specialists trained in empirical cleaning methodology to deliver log reductions in microbial contamination¸ so that our customers and customers customers can feel safe.

The Environmental Sanitization and Cleaning Solution delivered by Infection professionals comes with a SANIFIELD SAFE verification that means your space has been cleaned to the highest standard with suitable products.

Microbial resistance and healthcare-associated infections are two of the biggest threats in health care. They cause significant increases in patient morbidity and mortality and can increase the service delivery costs tenfold, not to mention the administrative, legal and socioeconomic burden.

It is for this reason that Infection Professionals customer focused sanitization and cleaning service is firmly based upon collaborative working relationships with our health industry clientele.

The costs of professional quality environmental sanitization and cleaning services are relatively minor. Cost-benefit-risk analysis clearly illustrates that investment in professional quality environmental sanitization and cleaning services is highly cost-effective. We know that with our infection professionals supervising small agile cleaning teams, environmental sanitization and cleaning is highly cost-effective.
